Everyday life, simplified

A community management system, with web and mobile applications with a connected and centralized database. The software suite includes smooth digital solutions for:

Visitor authorization

An in-app visitor request system and a dedicated verification app for security personnel.

Bill payments

An integrated system for payment of bills and rent through a few taps on your phone.

Admin announcement

Notice board feature on the resident app for admin to disperse important information.

Maintenance service provision

A repository of available and trusted maintenance providers for residents to access with ease.

Private social network for residents

A social platform just for the community members to discuss events, post queries & circulate news.

Venue booking

Scheduling and booking function to make reservations for venues and recreation centers.
